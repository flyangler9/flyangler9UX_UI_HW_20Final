The FireTeam Story

The UX UI team designed a responsive website for FireTeam. FireTeam delivers holisitic health outcomes to veterans by providing team-only holistic apps. This project delivered both mobile and desktop Hi-Fi prototypes stemming from intial UX research and application of UI design. The stakeholders adopted the prototypes for their use and endorsed the team for delivering an exceptional product.
